Sephora: Spec Campaign

For this school project, I created a spec campaign for Sephora to spotlight The INKEY List and challenge the idea that "skincare is just for women". My goal was to show that men care about their skin too. I used a curated set of The INKEY List products alongside stock imagery of men washing their faces, pairing each visual with product descriptions across digital, social, and magazine ad mockups. The overall tone is clean, inclusive, and approachable.
