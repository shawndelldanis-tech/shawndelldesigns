Technical Drawing & Print Ad Design

For this assignment, I created a precise technical drawing of a hair dryer, which I branded as "Blazé". The focus was on clean lines, proportion, and accuracy to capture the original product's form in a detailed vector illustration. I then incorporated the drawing into a full page print advertisement, combining layout, branding, and typography to create a polished, cohesive final piece. This project challenged me to balance technical precision with creative presentation.
