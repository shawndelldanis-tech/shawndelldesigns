Velvet Meadow: Unofficial Press Kit Design

For this spec assignment, I reimagined a press kit for Velvet Meadow using InDesign and Illustrator. The project is based on their 2024 album "Never Want to Leave My Head", a title and body of work already released by the band. I created original visuals for the album cover, vinyl packaging, and a collection of tour merchandise, blending their dreamy, nostalgic sound with a cohesive visual identity. I also pulled from some of my favorite existing tracks to build out a fictional track list and bring the mockup to life, while staying true to their aesthetic.
