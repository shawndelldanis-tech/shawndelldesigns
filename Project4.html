Mulholland Drive: Movie Poster Redesign

For this assignment, I created a reimagined movie poster for Mulholland Drive. The goal was to reinterpret the film’s themes and mood through a fresh visual concept. I designed a poster that captures the surreal, fragmented tone of the story with a focus on atmosphere, composition, and minimal color. This project allowed me to explore cinematic storytelling through graphic design while experimenting with symbolism and restraint.
