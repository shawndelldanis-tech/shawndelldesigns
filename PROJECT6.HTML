Cosmic Burst: Spec Pop Can Design

For this assignment, I created an original pop can design concept called "Cosmic Burst" a fictional berry flavored soda with a vibrant, space themed aesthetic. The label design was built entirely by me and formatted to fit a standard 16 FL Oz (473 mL) aluminum can. The overall design features a dreamy, muted blue palette with abstract shapes and a retro inspired typeface that adds a sense of fun while keeping things clean and balanced. Final deliverables included a full dieline layout and a 3D rendered mockup to visualize the product in context.
